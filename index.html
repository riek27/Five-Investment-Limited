FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our equipment hire, services, and how we can support your construction project.

What types of construction equipment do you hire out?

We offer a wide range of heavy machinery including excavators, graders, rollers, bulldozers, dump trucks, backhoes, wheel loaders, and more. All our equipment is well-maintained and ready for immediate hire.

How do I book equipment for hire?

Booking is easy! Call us at 0922 627 256 or 0980 204 357, email info@fiveinvestmentsltd.com, or visit our office in Rock City, next to the playground in Juba. Our team will confirm availability and arrange delivery.

What are your hire rates and payment terms?

Our rates are competitive and depend on the equipment type and hire duration. We offer flexible payment terms and can provide detailed quotes based on your specific project requirements. Contact us for a customized estimate.

Do you deliver equipment to project sites?

Yes! We provide equipment delivery to project sites across Juba and surrounding areas. Our team ensures safe transportation and on-time delivery so you can keep your project on schedule.

What condition is your equipment in?

All our equipment undergoes rigorous maintenance and regular inspections. We take pride in keeping our fleet in excellent working condition to ensure reliability and safety on every project.

Do you offer long-term hire options?

Absolutely! We offer flexible hire periods including daily, weekly, monthly, and long-term contracts. Longer hire periods often come with discounted rates. Talk to us about your project timeline and we'll create a tailored package.
